What is Leg Day?

Leg day! The day that many Bodybuilders, Powerlifters and fitness enthusiasts either love, hate, or have a strange love/hate relationship with. It's completely understandable why. Leg day demands a high level of mental focus, a never quit attitude and forces you to push through feelings of exhaustion, pain, dizziness and shortness of breathe.

While putting forth such effort, it is reasonable for us to want to make sure we are using exercises and strategies that are optimal for our goals. This is the reason this site exists. To help you the lifter get the most of of the toughest day, and reap the most rewards for your efforts. Here at leg day you'll find tips for selecting the optimal type of squat for your body type, a squat calculator, as well as video demos of how to correctly perform each squat variation.

Begin with the squat calculator below. It's results, combined with the type of lifter you are will help determine which squat variation is best for you. Happy Leg Day!

Leg Day Calculator

Calculator Info

One of the most helpful and important aspects of the Leg Day site is the squat calculator. It uses a formula to calculate your femur to overall height ratio. This helps determine which type of squat is optimal for you if you're a Powerlifter, and if free bar squatting is an optimal primary exercise for you, if you're a Bodybuilder. Armed with this information, you'll be able to explore areas of the site that fit your style of training.
