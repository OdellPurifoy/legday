What to use and why?

When training a particular body part, choosing the optimal exercises for our structure is not the only important factor. I highly recommend using lifting equipment such as wrist wraps, belts, knee sleeves and the apprporiate footware to get the most benefit from your leg training. Not only will these tools allow you to lift more efficiently and heavier, they'll also help minimize the risk for injury. Below are some gear suggestions that I think are beneficial to all lifters..

Lift belt

Key benefits

  • Provides lower back support.
  • Allows you to 'brace' against it to lift heavier.
  • Helps maintain neutral spinal alignment when lifting.

Knee Sleeve

Key benefits:

  • Provides support and stability to the knee joint
  • Provides more consistent and even support than knee wraps.
  • Can also be worn over the shins to protect legs during deadlifts.
